Some Basic Information about the table above
  • Non-Schengen visas can be renewed fairly quickly usually, if you wish to extend your stay.

  • Travel Cost may be able to be minimized a little more with a bit of research.
  • Closest travel was typically set to major centers within a 300km - 400km radius. Honestly, most of the cities with an airport offer lots of options for cheap travel.
  • Only cities with a good stable connection was used.
  • Only cities with a lower cost of living was used.
